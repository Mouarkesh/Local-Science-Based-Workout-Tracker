Home Screen
Your hub. If you have a routine scheduled for today it appears here ready to start with one tap. Otherwise hit Start Session for a quick empty workout.
Your progress stats and 28-day consistency grid live here too — check in after every session to watch them build.
📋
Routines
Create your own routine or load a template — Full Body, Push/Pull/Legs, Upper/Lower and more. Assign days of the week so it shows on your home screen automatically.
Tap Routines → Programme Templates to browse ready-made science-based programmes and load them in one tap.
🏋️
Logging a Session
Start a session and tap any exercise to open it. You'll see a recommendation based on your last performance — weight or reps to aim for.
Log your weight and reps for each set, tick the ✓ when done, then rate how it felt. Hit Save Exercise and move to the next.
📈
Progressive Overload
After every session the app recalibrates. Hit your rep target → next session it pushes the weight up. Miss it → it holds or deloads. Rate it "Easy" → it jumps further.
↑ Progress — hit target, moving up
→ Hold — partial, repeat same weight
↓ Deload — missed twice, weight drops 10%
✅
That's it.
Show up, log your sets, let the app tell you what to do next. Consistency beats everything.
The more you log, the smarter the recommendations get. Start with one routine and build from there.
